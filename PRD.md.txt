# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name
Mobile Billing Web App for Chicken Shop Owner

---

## Problem Statement
Small shop owners rely on paper bill books, which:
- Are hard to track
- Cause disputes
- Make pending amounts unclear
- Are easily lost or damaged

---

## Product Goal
Digitally replace the paper bill book without changing the owner’s workflow.

---

## Target User
- Single chicken shop owner
- Uses manual billing
- Uses mobile phone daily
- Needs simple, fast, reliable records

---

## User Goals
- Record customer details once
- Create bills quickly
- Track pending balances
- Access old bills anytime

---

## Functional Requirements

### Authentication
- Single owner login

### Customer Management
- Add customer
- View customer list
- Select customer while billing

### Billing
- Auto bill number
- Editable date
- Bird type selection
- Auto calculations
- Save and view bill

### Ledger
- Customer-wise bill history
- Total billed
- Total paid
- Pending balance

### Payments
- Add payment
- Update balance
- Preserve historical bills

---

## UX Requirements
- Mobile-first
- App-like feel
- Minimal text
- Large buttons
- No clutter

---

## Constraints
- No extra features
- No multi-user support
- No inventory

---

## MVP Definition
If the owner can fully stop using the paper bill book, the MVP is successful.
